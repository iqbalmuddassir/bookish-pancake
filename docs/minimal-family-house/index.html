Reference board

Approved visual target for the trapezoid plot — site, elevations, and floor plans. Detailed SVGs in the other tabs follow this layout.

FIG. 00 — Reference presentation board (site · elevations · GF · FF · rooftop).

What this board locks

  • Trapezoid: front 64′, rear 76′, sides 50′; road on the left (west)
  • Two-car parking, front lawn, rear garden with play, patio off dining
  • GF guest en-suite + living / dining / kitchen + powder
  • FF master, junior master, children room, play room, family lounge + balcony
  • Rooftop pergola sit-out, outdoor dining, BBQ, kitchen garden

Design brief

Minimal massing, family-first planning, productive greenery for fresh air and home-grown food.

Program

  • Ground: foyer, living, dining, kitchen + pantry, powder, guest + en-suite, stair
  • First: master + WIC + bath, junior master + bath, children + bath, play room, family lounge + balcony
  • Roof: pergola sitting, outdoor dining, BBQ, kitchen garden, stair head, utility
  • Site: gate, 2-car parking, front lawn, rear garden + kids play, patio

Assumptions

  • Plot ≈ ((64 + 76) / 2) × 50 = ~3,500 sq ft
  • Working setbacks: front/road 10′, rear 8′, sides 5′ (confirm locally)
  • Proposed built-up G+1 ≈ 2,450 sq ft under ~2,530 permissible
  • RCC frame, flat terrace, white plaster + stone + wood accents

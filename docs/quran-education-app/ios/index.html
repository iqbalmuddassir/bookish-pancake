Inside version 1.0

Everything for a mindful day

The first release brings a focused home hub and three self-contained tools — the same spirit as Android, designed natively for Apple.

Calm home hub

Time-aware greeting, next-prayer countdown, and one-tap continue listening so you pick up your recitation without hunting for the right surah.

Quran audio & translation

Urdu and English audio with translations. Browse by surah and rukoo, search, bookmark, download for offline play, and resume from the home screen.

Prayer times + widget

Location-based daily times calculated on-device with Adhan. A home-screen Next Prayer widget keeps the countdown on your Lock Screen glance path.

Halal E-codes

Search food additive codes for halal, haram, or doubtful status — clear labeled chips, VoiceOver-friendly, from a bundled offline database.

Look & feel

Quiet mosque courtyard

Sage and brass tones, soft surfaces, and generous space — light and dark modes included.
